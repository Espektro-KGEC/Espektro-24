ESPEKTRO is the Annual Cultural and Technical spectacle hosted by Kalyani Government Engineering College. 

As West Bengal's second-largest fest, Espektro masterfully intertwines the realms of technology and creativity, setting the stage for a multifaceted celebration. Daytime activities buzz with the excitement of technical competitions and innovative games, drawing in budding engineers and tech enthusiasts alike. As dusk approaches, the ambiance shifts to a more artistic tone. Evenings come alive with mesmerizing performances from some of India's most distinguished artists, showcasing a blend of traditional and contemporary talents.
